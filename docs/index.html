Bolster, AAR compliant

The bolster has openings to fit all types of truck-mounted brakes and dead lever space for body-mounted brakes. The center bowl uses an AAR compliant wear resistant ring. CCSB mounting surfaces can house any AAR side bearing designs.


Parameters:

  • Facilitates control of defects by inspectors in operation
  • Premium surface quality
  • Special B+ chemistry with higher mechanical properties
  • Internal solidity and extra clean steel
  • Best weight and tolerances
  • Optimized within M-202 standards

Side Frame,
AAR compliant

The unit guides with wear-resistant liners are designed to fit all types of truck-mounted brakes and body-mounted brakes. The columns use wear plates of an AAR design. The pedestal windows are also made to AAR standards and can house adapters with metal wear resistant pedestal roof liners or adapters with elastic pads.
